Vladimir Iashin

I am a researcher of deep learning who specializes in designing neural nets for video content understanding. I enjoy working at the intersection of deep learning, computer vision, sound, and natural language processing.

I completed my PhD in EECS at Tampere University, supervised by Esa Rahtu. I hold an MSc in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science, along with a BSc in Economics from HSE University.

Beyond my research, I enjoy hitting the gym and searching for terroir in coffee.
